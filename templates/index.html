Post Harvesting Process

Traditional husk peeling

Traditional arecanut husk peeling involves manually splitting the husk using a specialized iron blade or knife, requiring skill and precision.

Husk Peeling Using Machine

Arecanut husk peeling using a machine involves mechanical blades or rollers that automatically remove the husk efficiently and quickly.

Boiling of Arecanut

Arecanut boiling involves immersing peeled arecanuts in hot water for processing, enhancing flavor, and preparing them for drying or further use.

Drying and Grading

Arecanut drying involves placing peeled and boiled arecanuts under the sun for 7-10 days to reduce moisture content and preserve them.
